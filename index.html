3. A REDRAW OF AN OLDER PIECE THAT'S ALSO SUPER COOL!!

Medium: IbisPaint X

THIS ONE IS KINDA A LONG STORY, A GOOD ONE THOUGH. I DID THE ORIGINAL PIECE FOR AN ART CONTEST BACK IN HIGHSCHOOL. MY ARTSTYLE WAS SUPER DIFFERENT BACK THEN AND I HAD LITTLE CONFIDENCE IN MY USE OF COLORS. HOWEVER IN THIS NEW PIECE, THE COLORS IMPROVED DRASTICALLY AND I COULD MAKE SENSE OF THE IDEAS MORE EASILY. THE OG PIECE WAS SUPPOSED TO BE THE SHEPE CHARACTER GETTING CHASED BY A MUCH LARGER AND MUCH SCARIER WOLF-GUY... HE GOT CORNERED AND THE REST IS HISTORY.. THOUGH I FEEL LIKE THE IDEA CHANGED A LITTLE WITH THIS NEW ONE, IT'S AS IF THE CHASE DID HAPPEN AND THE SHEEP SOMEHOW SURVIVED. AND YEARS LATER, HE GETS REMINDED OF THAT ONE TRAUMATIZING TIME HENCE THE SHADOWY AND VAGUE FIGURE OF THE WOLF... CRAZY IDEA RIGHT? ENOUGH YAPPING!!
